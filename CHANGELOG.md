## [0.1.0] - 2026-07-19

- Released the MuJoCo model, velocity and motion-tracking tasks, BONES-SEED
  retargeting tools, ONNX export, sim2sim, and physical deployment programs.
- Added reference velocity and general-motion preview model artifacts with
  checksums and source-run provenance.
- Added validated per-robot Dynamixel mapping and neutral calibration profiles.
- Added automated lint, type, test, and package-build checks.
- Licensed the project under the MIT License while retaining upstream notices.
